View Projects Get in Touch

A licensed Professional Engineer with over three decades of progressive experience in public infrastructure planning, design, and construction management.

I began municipal engineering with 14 years in private consulting at Van Cleef Engineering Associates, providing water and wastewater engineering services to municipalities in New Jersey and Pennsylvania. Since joining the City of Wilmington in 2001, I have served in increasingly responsible roles — from Project Engineer to Director of Engineering — overseeing more than $250 million of public infrastructure design and construction during the last decade.

Capital Project Management

Over $250M in public infrastructure capital projects since 2014 including streets, buildings, parks, bridges, trails, and stormwater systems.

Water & Wastewater Engineering

Pumping, storage, distribution, collection, treatment, and disposal system design for public utility systems.

Municipal Engineering

Facilities master plans, capital improvement plans, technical standards, budgets, strategic business plans, and rate schedule development.

Construction Management

Construction inspection, contract administration, Right-of-Way permitting, and quality assurance for public infrastructure and private development.

Engineering Software

Computer modeling for infrastructure analysis and design using WaterCAD, AutoCAD 14, KY Surge, SketchUp, and Lumion. Proficient in Microsoft Excel, Word, PowerPoint, and Outlook.
