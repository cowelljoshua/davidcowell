About

A licensed Professional Engineer with over three decades of progressive experience in public infrastructure planning, design, and construction management.

Since joining the City of Wilmington in 2001, I have served in increasingly responsible roles — from Project Engineer to Director of Engineering — overseeing more than $250 million in capital projects spanning streets, public buildings, parks, bridges, trails, riverfronts, and stormwater infrastructure.

Prior to municipal service, I spent 14 years in private consulting at Van Cleef Engineering Associates, providing water and wastewater engineering services to municipalities across New Jersey and Pennsylvania.
