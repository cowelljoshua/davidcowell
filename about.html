About

A licensed Professional Engineer with over three decades of progressive experience in public infrastructure planning, design, and construction management.

I began municipal engineering with 14 years in private consulting at Van Cleef Engineering Associates, providing water and wastewater engineering services to municipalities in New Jersey and Pennsylvania. Since joining the City of Wilmington in 2001, I have served in increasingly responsible roles — from Project Engineer to Director of Engineering — overseeing more than $250 million of public infrastructure design and construction during the last decade.
