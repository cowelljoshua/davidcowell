\section{Professional Summary}

Licensed Professional Engineer with over 30 years of progressive experience in public infrastructure planning, design, and construction management. Since joining the City of Wilmington in 2001, served in increasingly responsible roles---from Project Engineer to Director of Engineering---overseeing more than \$250 million of public infrastructure design and construction.
